What We Do

End-to-end manufacturing capabilities for projects of any complexity

Precision CNC Routing

Large format CNC routing for all non-ferrous materials up to 18'' in height. We can process a variety of projects from sheet good processing to complex 3D composite molds.

CAD Design, Programming & CNC Machining

High precision CNC machining for parts that require +/- 0.001" accuracy in all materials. We can assist in the development of 3D models and designs for a variety of projects from consumer products to custom machinery.

Prototyping & Molds

Assist in the design and development of prototypes for consumer products, machinery components and other custom applications. Consultation services for material selection, cost reduction and increased quality.

We offer mold design and manufacturing for composites including carbon fiber, fiberglass and more. We can machine large complex 3D molds from high density foam, epoxy tooling board, aluminum and more.

Built on Precision
